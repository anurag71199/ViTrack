About The Project

ViTrack is your personal healthcare companion. Tracking is made easy using an OCR system based on CNN which lets you capture an image of your medical devices to store your readings. ViTrack provides tracking of 5 vital signs; heart rate, oxygen saturation, temperature, systolic blood pressure, and diastolic blood pressure. A comprehensive EWS (Early Warning Score) is calculated using machine learning models of the data of 30 days, which determines your overall health score. Apart from the health analysis provided by ViTrack, a doctor-patient portal is also offered, which lets you consult a doctor and get feedback on the reports that you upload. ViTrack has a companion app, which lets you easily scan and upload your readings, as well as be up-to-date on your health, on the go!
